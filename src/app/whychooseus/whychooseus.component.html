Solutions we provide

Inventory Reporting

We ensure that our team is well trained and familiar with the processes involved in documenting inventory in the first place. We use accurate and up-to-date technology to streamline the process. We perform regular audits to check for any discrepancies.

Riders Safety

We make sure all drivers are professional and haven’t engaged in any illegal activities by asking them to upload their character police certification.

On-Time Deliveries

We make sure drivers are being sent with proper route planning, or with the updated tracking system. If there’s a case which is not in our control like traffic jam on the highway, we inform our customers on prior basis and give them updates along the way.

Status of Consignments

We have proper tools available that helps us to track the consignment and get visibility into the status of the delivery. Our consignment tracking tools feature gives real-time updates on the status of the package.
