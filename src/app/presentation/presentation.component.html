Introduction

We commenced operation in 2018 in Pune, with a commitment of embracing sustainability, and today we are one of the integrated service providers. By following the concept of “Go Green”, TTD Logistics started functioning with a set of 20+ EVs and today we are leading the industry with 300+ EVs in the market already. We’re dedicated to delivering seamless logistics solutions tailored to your needs. With our extensive experience and commitment to excellence, we ensure your consignment reaches its destination safely and on time. In such a short span of time we managed to establish our brand in the market. TTD Logistics offers an extensive range of technology enabled logistics solutions to serve a wide scale of customers across diverse industry segments. It has transformed into a recognized brand offering a wide range of integrated delivery and transport solutions to customers and businesses. Our solutions range from traditional pick-up and delivery to integrated warehousing and order fulfillment allowing our customers optimize for time sensitivity and cost effectiveness.
We are also offering professional and efficient 3PL solutions with real time visibility, focusing on marketing, growth and customer satisfaction. We store the inventory, pick, pack and ship the product and provide a vital link between operations and customers.
