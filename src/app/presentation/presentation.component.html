History

In TTD Logistics dedication is the key that drives growth and our partnership is based on trust

TTD Logistics Pvt. Ltd. began operations in October 2018 and is headquartered in Pune, Maharashtra. It was founded by Mrs. Shrutika Sahebrao Rautwar with a vision to establish a company that will provide critical efficiency and reliability, and today it’s a popular logistics service provider in Pune that focuses on connecting people and improving lives. It offers secure and reliable delivery of consignments to multiple locations in India. We have our branches in over 6 cities till now and it aims to expand more not only in Pan India level but Globally.

Logistics management is a critical function that affects the efficiency, cost and customer satisfaction of any business and our team’s goal is specifically measured to achieve it.

Solutions we provide:


Inventory Reporting: We ensure that our team is well trained and familiar with the processes involved in documenting inventory in the first place. We use accurate and up-to-date technology to streamline the process. We perform regular audits to check for any discrepancies.


Riders Safety: We make sure all drivers are professional and haven’t engaged in any illegal activities by asking them to upload their character police certification.


On-Time Deliveries: We make sure drivers are being sent with proper route planning, or with the updated tracking system. If there’s a case which is not in our control like traffic jam on the highway, we inform our customers on prior basis and give them updates along the way.


Status of Consignments: We have proper tools available that helps us to track the consignment and get visibility into the status of the delivery. Our consignment tracking tools feature gives real-time updates on the status of the package.
