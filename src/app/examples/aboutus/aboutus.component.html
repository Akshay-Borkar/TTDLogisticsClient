TTD Logistics Pvt. Ltd. began operations in October 2018 and is headquartered in Pune, Maharashtra.

It was founded by Mrs. Shrutika Sahebrao Rautwar with a vision to establish a company that will provide critical efficiency and reliability, and today it’s a popular logistics service provider in Pune that focuses on connecting people and improving lives.

It offers secure and reliable delivery of consignments to multiple locations in India.

We have our branches in over 6 cities till now and it aims to expand more not only in Pan India level but Globally.

“In TTD Logistics dedication is the key that drives growth and our partnership is based on trust”

Logistics management is a critical function that affects the efficiency, cost and customer satisfaction of any business and our team’s goal is specifically measured to achieve it.

As we are growing since 2018, here’s a look at some of the milestones along our journey.

2018 - 2019:

TTD Logistics was established in the year 2018 and within the span of one year we managed to become one of the recognized brands in the market.

2020:

Received recognition award from one of the leading organizations in the logistics industry.

2023 - 2024:

We started operating from multiple locations in Maharashtra.
